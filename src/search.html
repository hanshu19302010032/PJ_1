Emirates Stadium

Emirates Stadium, also translated as Emirates Stadium or Amir stadium, is the home of Arsenal in England Football Super League, which can accommodate more than 60000 people. The stadium was officially opened in 2006-07 season.




The American Scene

American Travels: now it is possible to correctly evaluate the impact of travel on Alexei de Tocqueville's political and sociological thoughts. His study of various nationalities and their systems contributed to his works and all his political actions. Therefore, his travel diary should occupy an important position in his works. Just as Aristotle wrote at the end of Greek classical times, Bodin conceived the six books of the Republic in the early modern times, Montesquieu published the spirit of law at the end of absolute monarchy in France, and Tocqueville wrote his political philosophy in a changing era. It was during this period that the consequences of industrialization in the 19th century began to emerge; social classes, the state and all human life were being reshaped in a huge melting pot.
